Metis is a next generation D&D campaign management bot for Discord. It is designed to be easy to use and powerful.

What does this Bot do?

Ever wanted to manage all the different campaigns going on your Dungeons and Dragons server? Fret not! Metis is here to help.

Manage Campaigns

Campaigns are all displayed in one channel and players can join them easily by clicking a button!

Create Campaign

Users can fill out a form to create their own campaign.

Play Music

Our Bot has a custom music player where you can play music from YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, and more! Using a website, app or program, you can control the music from anywhere.

  • You can play music from YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, and more!
  • Play from anywhere using a website, app or program!
  • You can create playlists and add songs to them!

And much more!

  • Roll dice with the /roll command!
  • Makes checks with the /check command!
  • Quote someone!

Getting started

To get started, you need to invite the bot to your server. You can do this by clicking the button below.

Afterwards run the /help command to begin the setup.
